Frequently Asked Questions

CAN I CANCEL ANYTIME?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. All that we ask is that you cancel at least 72 hours before your next expected delivery date.
IS THERE A MINIMUM ORDER?
Yes. The minimum order is $35
DO YOU DELIVER TO A P.O. BOX?
No. FedEx requires a physical address in order to deliver perishables.
DO I HAVE TO BE HOME TO RECEIVE MY MEALS?
No. If no one is home at the time of delivery, FedEx will leave the package outside your door. However, we recommend that you pick up your package as soon as possible to prevent unwanted thawing.
WHAT IS THE BEST WAY TO STORE THE MEALS?
It is always best to store the meals in the freezer and keep only 1 or 2 days worth of food in the refrigerator to thaw out. Our meals will last up to 8 weeks in the freezer, and up to 1 week in the fridge.
DO YOU ACCEPT RETURNS?
Because we produce a perishable product, we CANNOT accept returned food items. We will promptly issue a replacement credit for any food item or meal that was damaged, spoiled, or impaired as a direct result of shipping or delivery.
